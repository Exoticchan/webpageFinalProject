What is a TUMBLEBUS?

A TUMBLEBUS is a gym on wheels filled with fun equipment--bringing the best possible physical education class to children at daycares, elementary schools, private schools, etc. Cheerfully decorated, the TUMBLEBUS provides a unique and fun way for children to receive the physical fitness that is so important to their development. Instructors "with a special heart toward children" make the experience memorable as well as educational.

TUMBLEBUS programs are for children, walking toddler to eight years old. TUMBLEBUS classes assist in building self-esteem as they accomplish each new physical skill in the lesson plan. TUMBLEBUS is a national program. We are happy to report that over 300 buses have been manufactured and currently operate throughout the United States!

Child's Rewards

Gymnastics is the foundation for many sports. The skills learned are the basis for all sports. They develop coordination, flexibility, strength, power, learn to listen and follow directions, gain self esteem, confidence, social interaction, rhythm, set goals, learn movement skills, and stay physically fit.
